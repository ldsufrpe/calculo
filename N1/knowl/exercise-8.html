Exercício 1.

Determine as integrais indefinidas abaixo:
a) \(\displaystyle\int 4 e^t \, dt\) = \(+ C\text{.}\)
b) \(\displaystyle\int 3^r \, dr\) = \(+ C\text{.}\)
c) \(\displaystyle\int \dfrac{10^x}{18} \,dx\) = \(+ C\text{.}\)
Resposta 1.
\(4e^{t}\)
Resposta 2.
\(\frac{3^{r}}{1.09861}\)
Resposta 3.
\(\frac{10^{x}}{18\cdot 2.30259}\)
Solução.
\begin{equation*} \int 4 e^t \, dt = 4 \int e^t \, dt = 4 e^t + C. \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \int 3^r \, dr = \dfrac{3^r}{\ln 3} + C. \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \begin{array} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{10^x}{18} \, dx = \dfrac{1}{18} \displaystyle\int 10^x \, dx = \dfrac{10^x}{18 \ln 10} + C. \end{array} \end{equation*}
