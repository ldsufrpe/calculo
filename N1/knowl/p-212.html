\begin{equation*} y(x) =\int 5^x \, dx = \dfrac{5^x}{\ln 5} + C \end{equation*}

Encontre \(C\) por definição \(y(3) = -3\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} \dfrac{5^{3}}{\ln 5} + C \amp =\amp -3 \\ C \amp =\amp -3 - \dfrac{5^{3}}{\ln 5} \approx -80.6668. \end{array} \end{equation*}
