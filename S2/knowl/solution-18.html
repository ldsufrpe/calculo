Solução 1.17.b.1.

A escolha \(x=\tan{u}\) implica em

\begin{gather} \dd x= \left(\frac{1}{\cos^2{u}}\right)\dd u \tag{\(\star\)} \end{gather}
\begin{gather} \sqrt{1+x^2} = \sqrt{1+\frac{\sin^2{u}}{\cos^2{u}}}=\sqrt{\frac{\cos^2{u}+ \sin^2{u}}{\cos^2{u}}}=\frac{1}{\cos{u}} \tag{\(\star\star\)} \end{gather}

De (\(\star\))–(\(\star\star\)) segue que

\begin{align*} \integral{\sqrt{1+x^2}}{x}\amp=\integral{\frac{1}{\cos{u}}}{u} \end{align*}
