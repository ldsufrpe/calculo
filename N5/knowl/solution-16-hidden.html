Àrea

\begin{equation*} \displaystyle =\int_{\ln{2}}^{\ln{4}} \left [ e^{2x}-e^{x}\right]\; dx = \left[\frac{e^{2x}}{2}-e^{x}\right]_{\ln{2}}^{\ln{4}} =\left( \frac{{4}^{2}}{2}-4\right)-\left(\frac{{2}^{2}}{2}-2\right)=4 \end{equation*}

A área entre as curvas é \(4\text{.}\)
