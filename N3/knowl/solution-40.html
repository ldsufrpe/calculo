Solução 1.8.27.1.

Solução
(a) \(F(9)=\int^{9}_{9} \sqrt{t^{2}+19}\;dt=0\text{.}\)
(b) \(F'(x)=\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}\int^{x}_{9} \sqrt{t^{2}+19}\;dt=\sqrt{x^{2}+19}\;\text{,}\) \(\;F'(9)=10\text{.}\)
(b) \(F''(x)= \frac{d}{dx} \left[\sqrt{x^{2}+19}\right]=\frac{x}{\sqrt{x^{2}+19}}\;\text{,}\) \(\;F''(9)={\textstyle\frac{9}{10}}\text{.}\)
