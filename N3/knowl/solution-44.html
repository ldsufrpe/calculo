Solução 1.8.33.1.

Seja \(u = x^2\text{.}\) Então \(du = 2x \, dx\) logo
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rl} \displaystyle \int_{0}^{3} x f(x^2)\,dx \amp = \displaystyle \int_0^{9} f(u) \left(\frac{1}{2} du\right) \\ \amp = \displaystyle \frac{1}{2} \int_0^{9} f(u)\, du \\ \amp = \displaystyle \frac{7}{2} \end{array} \end{equation*}
