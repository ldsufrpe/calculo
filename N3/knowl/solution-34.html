Solução 1.8.19.1.

Solução

\(u = 2 x - 5\text{.}\)

\(du = 2 dx\)

\(a = 2 \cdot 3 - 5 = 1\)

\(b = 2 \cdot 5 - 5 = 5\)

\(f(u) = \frac{1}{2} u^{4}\text{.}\)

O valor da integral original é:

\begin{equation*} \displaystyle \int_{3}^{5} (2 x - 5)^{4} dx = \int_{1}^{5} \frac{1}{2} u^{4} du = \frac{1}{2} \left[\frac{{u}^{5}}{5}\right]_{1}^{5} = \frac{1}{10} \left[(5)^{5} - (1)^{5}\right] = {\textstyle\frac{1562}{5}} \end{equation*}
