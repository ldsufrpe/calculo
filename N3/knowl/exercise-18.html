Exercício 5.

Determine as integrais definidas:
a) \(\displaystyle\int_{1}^{6} \dfrac{1}{x} \, dx =\)
b) \(\displaystyle\int_{1}^{4} \dfrac{1}{x^2} \, dx =\)
c) \(\displaystyle\int_{1}^{8} \dfrac{1}{x^{6}} \, dx =\)
d) \(\displaystyle\int_{0}^{8} x^2 \, dx =\)
e) \(\displaystyle\int_{0}^{1} x^{45} \, dx =\)
Resposta 1.
\(1.79175946922805\)
Resposta 2.
\(0.75\)
Resposta 3.
\(0.199993896484375\)
Resposta 4.
\(170.666666666667\)
Resposta 5.
\(0.0217391304347826\)
Solução.
Solução:
Use o Teorema Fundamental do Cálculo.
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} \displaystyle \int_{1}^{6} \dfrac{1}{x} \, dx \amp =\amp \left(\ln |x| \right) \Big|_{1}^{6}\\ \amp =\amp \ln(6) - \ln(1)\\ \amp =\amp \ln 6 \end{array} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} \displaystyle \int_{1}^{4} \dfrac{1}{x^2} \, dx \amp =\amp \left( -\dfrac{1}{x} \right) \Big|_{1}^{4}\\ \amp =\amp -\dfrac{1}{4} + 1 \amp =\amp 0.75 \end{array} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} \displaystyle \int_{1}^{8} \dfrac{1}{x^{6}} \, dx \amp =\amp \left(-\dfrac{x^{-5}}{5} \right) \Big|_{1}^{8}\\ \amp \amp \\ \amp =\amp -\dfrac{(8)^{-5}}{5} + \dfrac{1}{5} \\ \amp \amp \\ \amp =\amp 0.199994 \end{array} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} \displaystyle \int_{0}^{8} x^2 \, dx \amp =\amp \left( \dfrac{x^3}{3} \right) \Big|_{0}^{8}\\ \amp \amp \\ \amp =\amp \dfrac{(8)^3}{3} - 0\\ \amp \amp \\ \amp =\amp 170.667 \end{array} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} \displaystyle \int_{0}^{1} x^{45} \, dx \amp =\amp \left( \dfrac{x^{46}}{46} \right) \Big|_{0}^{1}\\ \amp \amp \\ \amp =\amp \dfrac{(1)^{46}}{46} - 0\\ \amp \amp \\ \amp =\amp \dfrac{1}{46} \end{array} \end{equation*}
