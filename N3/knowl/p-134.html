A escolha u=5-x, fornece \(\dd u=(-1)\dd x \text{.}\) Além disso, quando \(x=1\text{,}\) \(u=4\text{,}\) e , quendo \(x=3\text{,}\) \(u=2\text{.}\) Então,

\begin{align*} \integrald{\frac{1}{5-x}}{1}{3}{x} \amp = \integrald{-\frac{1}{u}}{4}{2}{u}\\ \amp = -\ln{u}\Biggr\rvert_4^{2} = - (\ln{2}-\ln{4})\\ \amp = \ln{\frac{4}{2}} = \ln{2} \approx 0,6931 \text{.} \end{align*}
