Solução 1.31.1.

Escolha \(u=x^2\text{.}\) Então, \(\frac{\dd u}{\dd x}=2x\) e \(\dd u= 2x\dd x\text{.}\) Quando \(x=0\text{,}\) \(u=0\text{,}\) e, quando \(x=2\text{,}\) \(u=4\text{.}\) Logo,
\begin{align*} \integrald{xe^{x^2}}{0}{2}{x}\amp = \integral{\frac{1}{2}e^u}{u} = \frac{1}{2}e^u \Biggr\rvert_0^{4}\\ \amp = \frac{1}{2}\left(e^4-e^0\right)=\frac{1}{2}(e^4-1)\\ \amp \approx 26.79 \text{.} \end{align*}
