Exercício 1.

Determine as integrais indefinidas usando substituição.
a) \(\displaystyle \int 3x^{2}\!\left(x^{3}-7\right)^{7} \, dx =\)
b) \(\displaystyle \int \left(2x+3\right)\!\left(x^{2}+3x+7\right)^{5} \, dx =\)
c) \(\displaystyle \int x\!\left(x^{2}+7\right)^{4} \, dx =\)
d) \(\displaystyle \int -\left(28x+6\right)\!\left(-\left(7x^{2}+3x+6\right)\right)^{6} \, dx =\)
Resposta 1.
\(\frac{\left(x^{3}-7\right)^{8}}{8}+C\)
Resposta 2.
\(\frac{\left(x^{2}+3x+7\right)^{6}}{6}+C\)
Resposta 3.
\(\frac{\left(x^{2}+7\right)^{5}}{10}+C\)
Resposta 4.
\({\textstyle\frac{2}{7}}\!\left(-\left(7x^{2}+3x+6\right)\right)^{7}+C\)
Solução.
a) Escolha \(u = (x^3-7)\text{.}\) Então \(du = 3x^2 \,dx\text{.}\)
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} \displaystyle \int 3x^2 (x^3-7)^{7} \, dx \amp =\amp \displaystyle \int u^{7} \, du \\ \amp =\amp \dfrac{1}{8} u^{8} + C\\ \amp =\amp \dfrac{1}{8} (x^3-7)^{8} + C. \end{array} \end{equation*}
b) Escolha \(u = (x^2+3*x+7)\text{.}\) Então \(du = (2x + 3)\,dx\text{.}\)
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} \displaystyle \int (2x + 3)((x^2+3*x+7))^{5} \, dx \amp =\amp \displaystyle \int u^{5} \, du \\ \amp =\amp \dfrac{1}{6} u^{6} + C\\ \amp =\amp \dfrac{1}{6} ((x^2+3*x+7))^{6} + C. \end{array} \end{equation*}
c) Escolha \(u = (x^2+7)\text{.}\) Então \(du = 2x \,dx\text{.}\)
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} \displaystyle \int x (x^2+7)^{4} \, dx \amp =\amp \displaystyle \dfrac{1}{2}\int u^{4} \, du \\ \amp =\amp \dfrac{1}{2}\cdot\dfrac{1}{5} u^{5} + C\\ \amp =\amp \dfrac{1}{10} (x^2+7)^{5} + C. \end{array} \end{equation*}
d) Escolha \(u = (-(7*x^2+3*x+6))\text{.}\) Então \(du = -14 x - 3 \,dx\text{.}\)
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} \displaystyle \int (-(28*x+6)*[-(7*x^2+3*x+6)]^6) \, dx \amp =\amp \displaystyle \int 2(-14 x - 3)((-(7*x^2+3*x+6)))^{6} \, dx \\ \amp =\amp \displaystyle 2\int u^{6} \, du \\ \amp =\amp 2\cdot\dfrac{1}{7} u^{7} + C\\ \amp =\amp (2/7) ((-(7*x^2+3*x+6)))^{7} + C. \end{array} \end{equation*}
