Solução 1.3.2.1.

a) Escolha \(u = x - 5\text{.}\) Então \(du = 1 \,dx\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} \displaystyle \int \dfrac{1}{x - 5} \, dx \amp =\amp \displaystyle \int \frac{1}{u} \,du \\ \amp =\amp \ln |u| + C\\ \amp =\amp \ln |x - 5| = C. \end{array} \end{equation*}

b) Escolha \(u = 2 x + 5\text{.}\) Então \(du = 2 \,dx\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} \displaystyle \int \dfrac{9}{2 x + 5} \, dx \amp =\amp \displaystyle \dfrac{1}{2} \int \dfrac{9}{u} \, du \\ \amp =\amp \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot 9 \ln |u| + C\\ \amp =\amp (9/2) \ln |2 x + 5| + C\\ \end{array} \end{equation*}
