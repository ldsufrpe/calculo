Parágrafo

Usando frações parciais,

\begin{equation*} \frac{x^{2}+8}{x\!\left(x-2\right)^{3}}=\frac{A}{(x-2)}+\frac{B}{(x-2)^2}+\frac{C}{(x-2)^3}+\frac{D}{x} \end{equation*}

temos

\begin{equation*} \begin{cases} A+D=0\\ B-4A-6D=1\\ 4A-2B+12D+C=0\\ -8D=8 \end{cases} \end{equation*}

produzindo

\begin{equation*} A=1,\;B=-1,\;C=6,\;D=-1 . \end{equation*}

Logo,

\begin{equation*} \int \frac{x^{2}+8}{x\!\left(x-2\right)^{3}} \;dx= \int \left( \frac{1}{x-2}-\frac{1}{\left(x-2\right)^{2}}+6\frac{1}{\left(x-2\right)^{3}}-\frac{1}{x}\right) \;dx=\ln\!\left(\left|x-2\right|\right)+\frac{1}{x-2}-\frac{3}{\left(x-2\right)^{2}}-\ln\!\left(\left|x\right|\right)+C \end{equation*}
