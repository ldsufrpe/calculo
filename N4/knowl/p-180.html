Parágrafo

Usando frações parciais,

\begin{equation*} \frac{x^{2}}{\left(x-7\right)^{3}}=\frac{A}{(x-7)}+\frac{B}{(x-7)^2}+\frac{C}{(x-7)^3} \end{equation*}

temos

\begin{equation*} \begin{cases} A=1\\ B-14A=0\\ C-\left(49A+7B\right)=0 \end{cases} \end{equation*}

produzindo

\begin{equation*} A=1,\;B=14,\;C=49 . \end{equation*}

Logo,

\begin{equation*} \int \frac{x^{2}}{\left(x-7\right)^{3}} \;dx= \int \left( \frac{1}{x-7}+\frac{14}{\left(x-7\right)^{2}}+\frac{49}{\left(x-7\right)^{3}}\right) \;dx=\ln\!\left(\left|x-7\right|\right)-\frac{14}{x-7}-\frac{49}{2\!\left(x-7\right)^{2}}+C \end{equation*}
