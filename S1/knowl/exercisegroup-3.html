[exercisegroup] 2.4.1–6.

Determine a função \(y=y(x)\text{,}\) \(x\in\mathbb{R}\text{,}\) para cada condição dada.

1.
\(\frac{\dd y}{\dd x}=3x-1\quad \text{e} \quad y(0)=2\)
2.
\(\frac{\dd y}{\dd x}=\cos{x} \quad \text{e} \quad y(0)=0\)
3.
\(\frac{\dd y}{\dd x}=x^3-x+1 \quad \text{e}\quad y(1)=1\)
4.
\(\frac{\dd y}{\dd x}=e^{-x} \quad \text{e}\quad y(0)=1\)
5.
\(\frac{\dd y}{\dd x}=\frac{1}{x^2}\quad \text{e}\quad y(1)=1\quad (x>0)\)
6.
\(\frac{\dd y}{\dd x}=3 + \frac{1}{x}\quad \text{e}\quad y(1)=2\quad(x>0)\)
